Alma's Recipe Book Contents: Easy chicken tagine Vegan burger American pancake Easy chicken tagine chicken tagine Time: 40 mins Serves: 10 mins Ingredients Quantity olive oil 2 tbsp chicken thighs 8 skinless boneless onion 1 chopped grated fresh root ginger 2 tsp saffron 1 pinch honey 1 tbsp carrot 400 g parsley small bunch lemon 1 to serve Step 1: Heat the oil in a large, wide pan with a lid, add the chicken, then fry quickly until lightly coloured. Add the onion and ginger, then fry for a further 2 mins. Step 2 Add 150ml water, the saffron, honey and carrots, season, then stir well. Bring to the boil, cover tightly, then simmer for 30 mins until the chicken is tender. Uncover and increase the heat for about 5 mins to reduce the sauce a little. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with lemon wedges for squeezing over. Vegan burger vegan burger Time: 5-10 mins Serves: 10 mins Ingredients Quantity shallot 1 chopped celery 1 stick parsley lesves 1 bunch can chickpeas , drained and left to dry a little 400 g garam masala 1-2 tsp tomato purée 1 tbsp plain flour 2 tbsp polenta , couscous, or dried breadcrumbs 1 tbsp oil, for frying 1 tbsp burger buns or pitta bread, lettuce, tomato, and sauces to serve - Step 1: Whizz the shallot, celery, parsley and most of the chickpeas to a coarse paste. Don’t overdo this, you want a texture slightly rougher than hummus. Mash the remaining chickpeas and stir them into the paste with the garam masala, tomato purée, flour and polenta. Season well. Step 2: Shape the mixture into four patties. Let them rest for at least 30 mins – you can leave them overnight in the fridge if you like. The polenta needs time to absorb any extra liquid. Step 3: Heat a little oil in a non-stick frying pan and cook the patties until they’re brown and crisp on each side. Try not to handle them too much as they’ll be quite soft when warm. Slide into buns or pitta with the accompaniments that you’d like. American pancake potato pancake Time: 30 mins Serves: 25 mins Ingredients Quantity self-raising flour 200 g baking powder 1,5 tsp colden caster sugar 1 btsp large eggs 3 melted butter 25 g milk 200 ml vegetable oil, for cooking - blueberry sauce - Step 1: Mix 200g self-raising flour, 1 ½ tsp baking powder, 1 tbsp golden caster sugar and a pinch of salt together in a large bowl. Step 2: Create a well in the centre with the back of your spoon then add 3 large eggs, 25g melted butter and 200ml milk. Step 3: Whisk together either with a balloon whisk or electric hand beaters until smooth then pour into a jug.
